This year, Oxbridge on Alssund 2018 will be held on Thursday, June 31 in the Port Basin immediately in front of Alsion as usual. We have received incredibly positive feedback from the total of 8 schools looking forward to having up to two teams each. We therefore expect approx. 60 young people who are going to fight about the hiking trophy that is currently held by Business College South (Det Blå Gym).
